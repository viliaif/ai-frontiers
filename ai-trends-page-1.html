Denmark Introduces Law to Protect Human Faces in the Age of AI

July 10, 2025

July 3, 2025

Meta has launched a new AGI lab under Yann LeCun and recruited top researchers from OpenAI, DeepMind, and Anthropic. High bonuses and internal friction at other orgs are fueling one of the most intense talent shifts in AI history.
