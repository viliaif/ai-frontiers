MIT Builds a Periodic Table for Artificial Intelligence

April 28, 2025

MIT researchers have developed a periodic system of machine learning algorithms, organizing over 20 classical methods into a map inspired by chemical elements. The system improves prediction accuracy by 8% and enables the design of new models that do not yet exist – they call it I-Con.
