AI Trends – Page 3

Sam Altman: We've crossed the event horizon – the soft singularity begins

June 18, 2025

Altman claims humanity has already stepped into the era of digital superintelligence. Unlike dramatic scenarios, the singularity is unfolding quietly and gradually. Instead of a shock, comes adaptation. “Miracles become routine, and then become basic requirements.” Altman calls for decentralization, ethics, and a global conversation about where we are headed.

MIT builds a periodic system for artificial intelligence

April 28, 2025

MIT researchers have created a periodic system of machine learning algorithms, structuring over 20 classical ML methods into a map resembling chemical elements. The system improves predictions, boosts accuracy by 8%, and enables development of yet non-existent models – named I-Con.

Xi Jinping calls for AI self-reliance amid rivalry with the U.S.

April 27, 2025

During a key Politburo meeting, Chinese President Xi Jinping urged technological independence in AI development. He emphasized innovation and protection of intellectual property.

Amazon and Microsoft slow down expansion of AI data centers

April 26, 2025

Due to massive energy consumption and regulatory pressures, tech giants are beginning to slow down the growth of AI infrastructure. Instead of expansion – comes consolidation and new ecological realism.
